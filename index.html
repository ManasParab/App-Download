Download To-Do Note App

Please follow the instructions below to download and install the app :

  1. Click the "Download" button below to download the APK file to your device.
  2. Open your device's Settings, navigate to "Security" or "Privacy", and enable "Unknown sources" or "Install unknown apps". This allows you to install apps from sources other than the Play Store.
  3. Once the download is complete, locate the APK file in your device's Downloads folder or the location where it was saved.
  4. Tap on the APK file to begin the installation process.
  5. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation.
